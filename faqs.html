Digital Food Scale
Question Answer
What is the maximum weight capacity of the Digital Food Scale? Our Digital Food Scale can measure up to 11 pounds (5 kilograms) with precision, making it perfect for everyday kitchen use.
Does the scale support different units of measurement? Yes! The scale allows you to switch between grams (g), ounces (oz), milliliters (ml), and pounds (lb) with the press of a button.
Is the surface easy to clean? Absolutely! The stainless steel platform is smooth and resistant to stains, making it easy to clean with a damp cloth.
Can I measure liquids with this scale? Yes, our scale supports liquid measurements in milliliters (ml) for water and milk, ensuring accurate results every time.
Does the scale have a tare function? Yes, the tare function allows you to zero out the weight of a container, so you can measure only the ingredients.
What type of batteries does the scale use? The scale operates on two AAA batteries, which are included with your purchase for immediate use.
